Malayalam Fonts

Nov 23, 2008
Tweet
When it comes to Malayalam fonts for web designers, there seems to be a lack of quality Malayalam fonts. However, if you observe Malayalam print and more traditional media, there are some very cool fonts being used in movie posters, news papers, magazines, and advertising. While doing a little searching for Malayalam fonts, I came across---"My Experiments with Type"---a presentation made at Typography Day 2008 IDC IIT Bombay in March 2008, by Malayalam font designer, Hashim P M, who currently is the Chief Designer at Desgin Difference Cochin.
Coming from an Engineering background, Design was like liberation for me. The joy of creation is overwhelming. And I fell in love with... type! I found that those little specks of black can make a very big impact. Photography, Printing, Publication Design, Graphics... Design gave me a whole new world. I have been enjoying every moment ever since.
Here are some of Hashim's fonts from his presentation:

Other Malayalam font resources

IFSCO Malayalam Font Catalog McGill University School of Computer Sc. List of Malayalam fonts. There are a lot of beautiful Malayalam and other Indic fonts out there, but good online resources for purchasing these fonts are lacking. I hope designers like Hashim will soon start to make some of their beautiful fonts available for web designers.
