Avial, Alternative Malayali Rock

Mar 06, 2008
Tweet
Inspired by Led Zeppelin, Frank Zappa, Pink Floyd, Rush and more, Avial---Our very own locally created in Kerala, "Alternative Malayali Rock Band". Their very first album released by Phat Phish Records, is now available for purchase at MusicYogi.
These guys have gone against the flow and taken a bold step by releasing an album in a regional language, Malayalam. They made an album that not only has catchy music but also engaged some the best wordsmiths of Kerala to write their lyrics.
If you want to check them out, they will be performing at Bolgati Palace, Kochi on March 8th.
