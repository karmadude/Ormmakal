--- layout: post title: Malayalam Fonts tags: - News status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: "1" --- When it comes to Malayalam fonts for web designers, there seems to be a lack of quality Malayalam fonts. However, if you observe Malayalam print and more traditional media, there are some very cool fonts being used in movie posters, news papers, magazines, and advertising. While doing a little searching for Malayalam fonts, I came across---"My Experiments with Type"---a presentation made at Typography Day 2008 IDC IIT Bombay in March 2008, by Malayalam font designer, Hashim P M, who currently is the Chief Designer at Desgin Difference Cochin.
Coming from an Engineering background, Design was like liberation for me. The joy of creation is overwhelming. And I fell in love with... type! I found that those little specks of black can make a very big impact. Photography, Printing, Publication Design, Graphics... Design gave me a whole new world. I have been enjoying every moment ever since.
