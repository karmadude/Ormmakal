--- layout: post title: SRAMPUT tags: - Video status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: "1" --- A documentary on Sri Ramuni Paniker Udbodhan Trust, which was established by the Spanish descendants of Ramuni Paniker to provide young students with financial assistance for higher education.
The Trust is also a real cooperation between people in the two far away territories (Kerala and Catalonia) where Ramuni Paniker lived and left his descendents.
