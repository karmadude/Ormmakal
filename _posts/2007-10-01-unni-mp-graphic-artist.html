Creativity comes from my mind, from my little knowledge; I am a villager, so I get lot of inspiration from my daily life
I am always on the lookout for artists from Kerala, and I am glad I chanced up on Alappuzha based graphic artist, Unni MP's fascinating Kerala style illustrations on flickr. They have a very rustic modern feel to them and very unique. Check out his flickr page for more from his collection of illustrations, photographs and other graphical work. Unni is 35 years old, with over 10 years of experience in advertising. He also runs a drawing coaching center for children under 12, and in his own words, "The main objective of the curriculum is to develop their skills in drawing, painting , how one can create innovative ideas, that will reflect in their character."
